  Market Participants:  Pursuant to the ISO's Governing Board decision in October1999 and FERC's ruling in March 2000
, the ISO has begun releasing market bidinformation, lagged by a six month period and without revealing the names ofbidders, beginning on August 1, 2000 for operating date February 1, 2000.Bid data will be provided for the ISO's ancillary service markets, real-timeenergy market, and congestion management market.  
These data can be found on the ISO's web site
at(http://www.caiso.com/marketops/OASIS/<http://www.caiso.com/marketops/OASIS/> ) and will be updated daily.
