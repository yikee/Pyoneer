  Market Participants and Schedule Coordinators,  Please be advised that all Supplemental Energy bids(including imports and exports) submitted to the ISO for use in thereal-time Imbalance Energy Market are considered to be "FIRM"
(i.e., thebids cannot be withdrawn after 45 minutes prior to the Settlement Period andcan be dispatched by the ISO anytime during the Settlement Period, see - ISOTariff o 2.5.22.4.1).  
Any bids labeled as "Non- Firm" or reported to the CAISOreal-time scheduler as "Non-Firm" or recallable will be rejected.  
The CAISOdoes not carry the necessary excess reserves to support "Non-Firm"Supplemental Imports.
